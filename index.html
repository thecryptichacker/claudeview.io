Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is ClaudeView?
ClaudeView is a desktop application that connects Anthropic's most powerful AI model with TradingView. You describe your trade strategy once, then open any stock or crypto chart and Claude analyzes it against your rules in real time, suggesting precise entry, exit, and stop levels. Think of it as a senior analyst sitting next to you, 24/7.
How does Claude know my strategy?
You describe how you trade in plain English the first time you open ClaudeView. Rules like "1H breakout with volume confirmation", "minimum 2R risk-reward", or "swing on the daily with EMA pullbacks". Claude saves your strategy and applies it to every chart you open, comparing your rules to live price action on that specific stock or crypto.
How does the $1 trial work?
$1 today gets you 3 full days of unlimited access to every feature in ClaudeView - less than the cost of a coffee to see if it fits the way you trade. If you love it, your subscription kicks in automatically on day 4 at the locked-in launch price. If you don't, cancel with one click before the trial ends and you won't be charged again.
Do I need a Claude subscription?
Yes. ClaudeView connects to your own Claude account, so an active subscription with Anthropic is required. The Claude Pro plan at $20/month is the minimum tier and works perfectly with every feature in ClaudeView. If you already pay for Pro, Max, or Teams, you're good to go.
Do I need a paid TradingView account?
No. ClaudeView works with both free and paid TradingView accounts. If you already pay for TradingView Pro or Premium, your higher-resolution charts and extended timeframes work seamlessly inside ClaudeView, but it's not required.
Which AI model does ClaudeView use?
ClaudeView is powered by Claude Opus, Anthropic's flagship reasoning model. We automatically upgrade to every new release the moment it's available, so you always have the sharpest, most capable AI working on your charts. No version management, no manual updates.

ClaudeView runs natively on macOS and Windows. Linux support is on the roadmap. The app is lightweight and runs alongside your existing TradingView workflow without any heavy resource use.
